Song of My Life

What Song Are You?

Song of My Life matches the things you say with the songs you'll like. Put in your Twitter handle (or one of your friend's!), and it will find a song that appears most fitting with the kind of stuff you post.

How Is It Done?

Song of My Life is built on top of two NLP algorithms: the RAKE (Rapid Automatic Keyword Extraction) from Rose et al., and the Word2Vec algorithm from Mikolov and Le. The site uses RAKE to extract keywords from your Twitter posts and various song lyrics, and uses Word2Vec to determine the similarity your posts' keywords and the keywords of a given song.
